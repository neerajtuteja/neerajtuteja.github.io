Surreal CMS in three steps

1. Connect your website

Connect your website to Surreal CMS and select the pages you want to edit. There’s no need to install anything and you never have to worry about updates. Our only requirement is an FTP/SFTP or Amazon S3 connection.

Setup is easy, and you can have things up and running in less than two minutes. Watch how it’s done in this quick video!

2. Add your clients

Give your clients access and let them safely edit their content. Surreal lets you define which parts of each page they can edit. Since all changes are tracked, you can always revert to a previous version if necessary.

Our inline editor is so easy to use, most clients don’t require any training to get started. Just click on the page and type—it’s really that easy!

3. Explore our features

Surreal comes with a ton of great features from our built-in Google Analytics Dashboard to scheduled publishing to our white-label features. We even offer an API so you can integrate with your own applications.

You’ve finally found the easiest way to edit content. Why not take a moment to test it out?
