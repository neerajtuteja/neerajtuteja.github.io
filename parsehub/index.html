Built for the modern web.

You can extract data from anywhere. ParseHub works with single-page apps, multi-page apps and just about any other modern web technology.

ParseHub can handle Javascript, AJAX, cookies, sessions and redirects. You can easily fill in forms, loop through dropdowns, login to websites, click on interactive maps and even deal with infinite scrolling.

Connecting the dots.

Don't waste any more time in your browser's web inspector trying to figure out how a webpage is structured.

ParseHub uses machine learning for its state-of-the-art relationship engine. It can understand intricate hierarchies of objects and how they fit together.

It can even understand recursive relationships, like how comments relate to their responses in a reddit thread.

Incredibly powerful.
