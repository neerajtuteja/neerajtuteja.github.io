Group Chat

Group chat replaces IRC & Skype or Google chats for your team and lets you quickly share files & thoughts. Transcripts are stored forever.

Team Inbox

The team inbox displays emails and messages from project management, customer support, source control & other tools. Keep your personal inbox clutter-free.

React Together In Real Time

By integrating your team's workflows into Flowdock, you'll have one place to discuss it all. React, decide and solve problems faster than ever.

1-On-1 Private Chat

Have something delicate to say? Just don't want to bother others? With Flowdock, you can chat 1 to 1.

Chat & Threads

Flowdock's unique combination of chat and threaded discussion gives you the best of both worlds.

Search All History

Search through all your chat transcripts. Let Flowdock remember for you, since they're stored forever.

From your desk to your pocket.

Wherever you go, on the device that you love

Keep Flowdock always with you. Any modern browser gives you Flowdock on the desktop, and our iOS (iPhone & iPad) and Android apps keep you up to date when on the move. Mac OS X and Windows users even get their own app. If your team includes any old-timers, they can use Flowdock with an IRC client.
