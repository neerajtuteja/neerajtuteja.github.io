The Problem

There are now over 1 million apps in the iOS App Store and growing. The average cost-per-install has reached a new-high of $1.92.

Whilst it's been reported that 25 developers earn 50% of the revenue, there are roughly 800,000 developers. Most of us don't have the cash to promote out applications through existing ad networks.

Our Solution

We are a peer-to-peer advertising network whereby users trade our digital currency called “Daq” instead of dollars, to lower the costs of acquiring a user.

Built by indie app developers, for indie app developers. When you signup, we give you 1,000 Daq to try out our platform so you can see the results yourself immediately.
