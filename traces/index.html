ABOUT

TRACES IS A MESSAGING APP THAT LETS YOU SHARE SECRET VIDEOS, PICTURES AND MUSIC THROUGH THE REAL WORLD. ONLY FRIENDS IN THE RIGHT PLACE AT THE RIGHT TIME CAN RECEIVE YOUR MESSAGE. THE APP WAS CONCEIVED BY NEUROSCIENTIST BEAU LOTTO, AS AN EXPERIMENT TO SEE HOW THE PRINCIPLES OF NEURODESIGN CAN BE HARNESSED TO CREATE MORE MEANINGFUL, HUMAN DIGITAL EXPERIENCES. THE TRACES TEAM INCLUDES LEADING RESEARCH SCIENTISTS, DESIGNERS, DEVELOPERS AND MOBILE INNOVATORS. TRACES IS PRIVATELY-FUNDED BY ANGELS ON THE WEST COAST AND IN THE UK.
